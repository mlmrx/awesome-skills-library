# Code of Conduct

Awesome Skills Library is intended to be a serious, practical open-source project for reusable AI agent capabilities. Contributors are expected to act with professionalism and respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas, designs, and implementations rather than people.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing carefully.
- Make space for contributors from different backgrounds and organizations.
- Keep discussions focused on project quality, safety, and maintainability.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained hostile behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately submitting unsafe, deceptive, or malicious skill content.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior undermines the project. Reports should be sent through the contact process listed in `SECURITY.md` when safety or abuse concerns are involved.
